Privacy Policy for the iOS App Geezmo

Effective Date: August 27, 2024

The Geezmo app (hereinafter referred to as the "App") places great importance on the privacy of its users. We strive to ensure transparency and protection of information related to the use of the App.

1. Data Collection

The Geezmo App does not collect or store any personal data, such as name, email address, phone number, or other identifying information about users.

2. Use of Data

The only information that may be collected and used is related to Google Analytics for the purpose of analyzing user behavior in the App and improving its functionality. This data is anonymized and does not contain any information that can identify the user.

3. Integration with Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze user interactions with the App. Google Analytics may collect anonymous information such as:

  • Time spent using the App;
  • Frequency of opening the App;
  • Device type and operating system;
  • Location at the country or regional level.

This data is used exclusively to improve the performance of the App and analyze its usage.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

The Geezmo App does not share data with third parties, except when necessary to perform functions related to the use of Google Analytics. All transmitted information is anonymized.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data collected through the App. However, as with any data transmitted over the Internet, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that you periodically review this page to stay informed of any changes.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding the Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at: jareksedy@icloud.com.
